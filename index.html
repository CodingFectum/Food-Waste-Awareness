OUR INTRODUCTION

Help end hunger by reducing your food waste

Food waste is a significant global issue. According to the Food and Agriculture Organization of the United Nations (FAO), approximately one-third of the food produced in the world for human consumption is lost or wasted each year. This equates to about 1.3 billion tons of food. Food waste has a negative impact on the environment and significant social and economic consequences, as it represents a waste of resources and potential food for those in need. Food waste occurs at all stages of the food supply chain, from production to consumption.
